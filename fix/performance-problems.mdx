<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Low open rate">
  - Test a new subject line (keep one control)
  - Send during business hours for your timezone
  - Reduce daily caps; warm reputation
  <Check>Open rate rises week over week.</Check>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Low reply rate">
    - Shorten the body; ask one clear question - Add one-line personalization to line 1 - Extend
    sequence with one extra follow-up
    <Check>Reply rate improves vs. prior week.</Check>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Routing issues (missing/late handoffs)">
  - Re-test forwarding rule from an external email
  - Verify Instantly → CRM/Airtable sync is on
  - Add a backup catch-all route
  <Check>Test replies appear in sales inbox within 1 minute.</Check>
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pause rules

-   Pause any campaign with bounce rate ≥ 3%
-   Pause campaigns if opens drop > 50% day-over-day
