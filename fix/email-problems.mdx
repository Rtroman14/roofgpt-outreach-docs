---
title: "Fix — Email problems"
description: "Quick fixes for send failures, high bounces, or spam placement."
---

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Can’t send emails">
  - Check inbox connection in Instantly; re-auth if needed
  - Verify SPF/DKIM/DMARC still passing
  - Ensure daily caps not exceeded
  <Check>Sending resumes without errors.</Check>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="High bounce rate (≥ 3%)">
    - Pause the affected campaign immediately - Re-validate the last imported leads; remove bad
    records - Lower daily caps for 72 hours, then resume
    <Warning>Continuing to send with high bounces hurts deliverability.</Warning>
    <Check>Bounce rate returns below 3% for 3 consecutive days.</Check>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Landing in spam">
  - Reduce daily caps and slow ramp
  - Swap subject/body variant; remove spammy words
  - Verify sending from multiple warmed inboxes
  <Check>Open rate improves and spam folder checks decline.</Check>
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## When to escalate

-   Authentication failing for > 24h
-   Multiple domains hit spam simultaneously
-   Bounce rate > 5% after list cleanup
