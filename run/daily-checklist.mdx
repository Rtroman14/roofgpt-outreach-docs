---
title: "Daily checklist"
description: "A 5‑minute routine to keep sending safe and leads flowing."
---

<Steps>
<Step title="Check health (1 min)">
  - SPF/DKIM/DMARC green in your dashboard
  - Bounce rate yesterday < 3%
  <Check>All lights green; no alerts.</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Review metrics (2 min)">
    - Opens, replies, positives in Airtable Dashboard - Spot dips > 20% day-over-day
    <Check>KPIs within normal range.</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Triage replies (1–2 min)">
    - Check AI classifications - Book inspections, assign owners, set follow-ups
    <Check>All Positive/Referral replies handled.</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Verify sending (1 min)">
  - Sequences active; limits not exceeded
  - Routing/forwarding works (spot test if needed)
  <Check>Sending shows Active; test mail received by sales.</Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>If any KPI is red, pause the affected campaign and go to Fix → Performance problems.</Tip>
