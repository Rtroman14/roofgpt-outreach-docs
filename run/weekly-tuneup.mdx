---
title: "Weekly tune‑up"
description: "A 15‑minute routine to improve deliverability and results."
---

<Steps>
<Step title="Rotate copy">
  - Swap in a fresh subject or opening line
  - Keep one control version for A/B tests
  <Check>At least one variant updated this week.</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Prune lists">
  - Remove bounces and long-term non-openers
  - Keep bounce rate < 3%
  <Check>List size reduced; quality improved.</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Ramp limits slightly">
    - Increase daily caps modestly if all green - Never more than a small weekly increment
    <Warning>Large jumps risk spam placement.</Warning>
    <Check>Caps increased safely with no errors.</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Review A/B results">
    - Keep winners; replace losers
    <Check>Winning variant remains active.</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Add new leads">
    - Import fresh Reonomy/CSV lists - Validate with NeverBounce
    <Check>New Valid prospects appear in the right campaigns.</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Glance at DMARC reports">
  - Confirm no widespread failures
  <Check>No anomalies found.</Check>
</Step>
</Steps>
