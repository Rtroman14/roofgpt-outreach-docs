## Prerequisites

-   Warmup complete for all inboxes
-   Sequences loaded and limits set

<Steps>
<Step title="Confirm warmup complete">
  - Check warmup dashboard for each inbox.
  <Check>All inboxes show Ready/Completed.</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Turn on sequences">
    - Enable sequences for assigned campaigns. - Start with conservative caps.
    <Warning>Never jump limits abruptly; ramp weekly.</Warning>
    <Check>Sending shows Active with no errors.</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Verify reply routing">
  - Send a test email and reply.
  - Confirm forwarding reaches the right rep.
  <Check>Test reply appears in the sales inbox and Airtable log.</Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

## What success looks like

-   Sends occurring daily within limits
-   Bounce rate < 3%
-   Positive replies logged and assigned
