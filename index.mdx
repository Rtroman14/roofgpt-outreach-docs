<Steps>
<Step title="Step 1 — Email setup">
  Set up your sending domains and inboxes.

<Tip>Plan 15-30 minutes.</Tip>

-   Buy lookalike domains
-   Create Google Workspace inboxes
-   Add SPF, DKIM, DMARC

<Frame>
    <img src="/images/roofgpt-domains.png" alt="List of domains" />
</Frame>

</Step>

<Step title="Step 2 — Instantly setup">
  Connect inboxes and load your sequences.

-   Connect each inbox to Instantly and start warming
-   Set safe sending limits and schedule
-   Create sequences and enable A/B tests

<Frame>
    <img src="/images/roofgpt-instantly.png" alt="Screenshot for Instantly setup" />

</Frame>

  <Check>Each inbox shows Connected and has limits set.</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Step 3 — Leads & Airtable">
  Get prospects into Airtable and validated.

-   Open the Airtable base
-   Add a Reonomy link or import CSV
-   Validate emails with NeverBounce
-   Auto-assign to Instantly campaigns

<Frame>
    <img src="/images/roofgpt-airtable.png" alt="Screenshot for Airtable" />
</Frame>

</Step>

<Step title="Step 4 — Launch">
  Start sending once warmup completes.

-   Confirm warmup reached target
-   Turn on sequences

</Step>
</Steps>
